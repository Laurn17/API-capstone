EVAMP is an app that connects you to events (EVamp) and campgrounds (evAMP) in a United States or Canadian city of your choice.

For US searches: Enter the city and State you'd like to visit.
For Canada searches: Enter the city followed by "Canada" -- Ex. Montreal, Canada.

Lastly, enter the day you plan to arrive and the day you plan to leave and search!
